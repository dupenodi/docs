## Experience Layer – Your Design Playground

Imagine having a digital canvas where you can create and customize your app’s front end without touching a single line of code. That’s what the Experience Layer is all about!

### Flow Builder

Think of Flow Builder as your app’s interior decorator. It lets you design stunning interfaces, map out user journeys, and test everything to perfection—all without coding. It’s like having a design studio right at your fingertips.

#### Card

A single screen in your app where all the magic happens. Each Card represents a snapshot of your app’s interface.

##### Card Creation Methods

- **Figma to Card Conversion Plugin**: Transform your Figma designs into Cards with just a few clicks. Your Figma files are now directly usable in your app!
- **Screenshot to Card Conversion**: Take screenshots and convert them into Cards instantly. It’s as simple as snapping a pic and getting back to work.
- **Prompt to Card Conversion**: Describe what you want, and watch Flow Builder turn your prompts into ready-to-use Cards. Just type and see the magic happen.

#### Journey

Map out user experiences with intuitive graphs. Visualize the entire user adventure with ease.

#### Simulate

Test your flow to ensure everything works perfectly before going live. It’s your chance to iron out any issues.

#### Gallery View

See all your Cards at once, just like flipping through slides in Google Slides. Get a comprehensive view of your design.

#### UI Checks

Ensure your design meets accessibility standards and other technical requirements. It’s like having a personal QA team to catch those pesky issues before they become a problem.

### UX Library

The UX Library ensures that the flows are not only beautiful but also consistent and efficient:

#### Themes

Use pre-defined theme variables like colors, fonts, and spacing from your design system, allowing you to maintain a cohesive look across your app effortlessly.

##### How to Create a Theme

You can create themes in various ways to match your design needs:

- **From Figma Design System**: Import your Figma design system directly to set up themes that align with your existing design guidelines.
- **From Prompt**: Simply describe your desired theme, and Loop AI will generate it for you, turning your vision into a brand-compliant theme.
- **From Screenshot**: Upload a screenshot of your design, and Loop AI will extract and create a theme based on the visual elements in the screenshot.

#### Element Variants

Create multiple variants of elements, such as buttons or input fields, which enhances flexibility and consistency in your design without reinventing the wheel.

#### Card Templates

Access pre-designed templates for Cards, speeding up your design process and ensuring consistency across different screens.

#### Element Components

Group related elements together to form reusable component groups, streamlining your design process and reducing errors with pre-made building blocks.

Putting It All Together

In the Experience Layer, Flow Builder and the UX Library work hand in hand to make your app design process smooth and efficient. Flow Builder allows you to create and test stunning user interfaces without coding, while the UX Library ensures that every element of your app stays cohesive and on-brand. With Card Creation and UI Checks, you’re equipped to build and refine your app’s front end with confidence.

Together, these tools help you deliver an engaging, consistent, and polished user experience from start to finish. Dive in and let LoopX’s Experience Layer transform your app development into a creative and hassle-free adventure!
